Applying for a Rental through Camelot Properties

    Please note our requirements state that you MUST:
  • make 3 time's the monthly rent
  • Pass a background and credit check (credit does not have to be perfect)
  • Have a positive rental history
  • Have NO evictions or open bankruptcies
  • Application fees are nonrefundable
  • Everyone 19 years and older must apply separately
    Be sure to submit your rental application along with:
  • 3 years of residential history
  • Contact information for your rental references.
  • Information on your monthly income, and please note that most properties require that applicant combined gross income is at least three (3) times the monthly rent amount.
  • $50 for Application fee ( Please Note that Each resident over the age of 18 must submit a separate rental application.)
